Introduction

I recently delivered a webinar for SilverlightShow titled “An Overview of the New Windows Phone 8 SDK.” The recording of the webinar is now available on-demand. Continue reading below for more information on what topics were covered.

The Windows Phone 8 SDK brings along many new features that could benefit your next Windows Phone app. Come take a look at several of these new features including: Tiles and Notifications Changing Lock Screen Speech Recognition and Multi-Resolution apps. We will also discover the additional tooling support and templates covered with this release. This is definitely a session to get you up to speed quickly with this new and exciting platform.
